Upcoming Events

Annual Ordination Service

March 13-15, 2026

Beloved family in Christ, we joyfully invite you to witness and celebrate a sacred milestone in the life of our church—the ordination of God’s chosen servant. Ordination is not just a ceremony; it is a divine affirmation of God’s call, a moment when the Church sets apart men and women to shepherd His people, proclaim His Word, and serve with humility and love. As hands are laid and prayers are lifted, heaven and earth unite to recognize the weight and wonder of this calling. Join us as we honor this holy appointment, rejoice in God’s faithfulness, and commit ourselves anew to the mission of Christ’s Church. Your presence will not only bless the one being ordained, but will also strengthen the entire body of believers as we walk together in faith.

Time: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Location: Main Sanctuary

Theme: "God's Abundant Provision"

Evangelism 2026

April 5-7, 2026

Dear brothers and sisters, we are excited to invite you to a life-changing season of evangelism! Evangelism is the heartbeat of the Church—it is the way we share the love of Christ and bring hope to a world in need. This gathering is not just about words, but about action: reaching out, touching lives, and shining the light of Jesus wherever darkness exists. Together, we will worship, pray, and boldly proclaim the Good News that transforms hearts and restores destinies. Your presence is vital, because every believer is called to be a witness. Come and be part of this divine mission, where heaven rejoices over every soul won for Christ.

Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM (Daily)

Location: Church Premises

Theme: "Arise and Shine"
