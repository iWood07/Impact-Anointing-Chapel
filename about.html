About Impact Anointing Chapel

Our History

Impact Anointing Chapel was established in 1990 with a divine mandate to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and bring salvation to the lost. For over three decades, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to winning souls and transforming lives through the power of God's Word.

From our humble beginnings, God has grown this ministry into a vibrant community of believers who are passionate about worship, prayer, and evangelism. Through faithful preaching, dedicated leadership, and the grace of God, Impact Anointing Chapel has touched countless lives and continues to be a lighthouse of hope in our community.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is clear and unwavering: Winning Souls and Salvation. We believe that every person deserves to hear the gospel and experience the life-changing power of Jesus Christ. Through evangelism, discipleship, and community outreach, we are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission.

We envision a church where lives are transformed, families are restored, and believers are equipped to be witnesses of Christ in their communities. Through sanctification and dedication to Christ, we strive to live holy lives that reflect God's glory.

Our Core Values

Sanctification

We believe in living holy lives set apart for God's purpose, pursuing righteousness through Christ.

Evangelism

Winning souls is at the heart of our mission. We are committed to sharing the gospel with all who will hear.

Prayer

We are a house of prayer, believing in the power of intercession and seeking God's presence continually.

Discipleship

We are dedicated to teaching and training believers to grow in their faith and become mature followers of Christ.

Community

We value fellowship and unity, creating a family atmosphere where everyone belongs and is loved.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do, offering our best to God in worship, service, and ministry.
