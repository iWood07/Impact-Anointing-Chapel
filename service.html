Services & Programs

At Impact Anointing Chapel, we offer a variety of services and programs designed to strengthen your faith, deepen your relationship with God, and equip you for ministry. Join us as we worship together and grow in the knowledge of Christ.

Sunday Worship Service

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Our main weekly gathering where we come together as a church family to worship God, receive the Word, and fellowship with one another.

What to Expect:

  • Anointed worship and praise
  • Powerful preaching from the Word of God
  • Corporate prayer and intercession
  • Altar call for salvation and healing
  • Fellowship and community

Friday Evening Service

6:00 PM - 8:30 PM

A special evening dedicated to seeking God's presence through prayer, worship, and the study of His Word. This service emphasizes spiritual breakthrough and divine encounters.

What to Expect:

  • Extended worship sessions
  • Teaching and biblical instruction
  • Prayer ministry and deliverance
  • Prophetic ministry
  • Holy Communion (select Fridays)

Covenant of God Missionary University

Impact Anointing Chapel is proud to host the Covenant of God Missionary University, a Bible school dedicated to training and equipping believers for effective ministry and Christian leadership. Under the guidance of Archbishop Dr. Frank Ed Wood, our Bible school provides comprehensive theological education rooted in sound biblical doctrine and practical ministry experience.

Whether you are called to pastoral ministry, missions, teaching, or marketplace ministry, our programs are designed to prepare you spiritually, academically, and practically for the work God has called you to do.

Biblical Studies

Comprehensive study of Scripture, theology, and Christian doctrine

Ministry Training

Practical training in preaching, teaching, and pastoral care

Spiritual Formation

Character development and spiritual growth for effective ministry

Leadership Development

Equipping students to lead churches and Christian organizations

Interested in enrolling? Contact the church office for more information about programs, admission requirements, and enrollment periods.
