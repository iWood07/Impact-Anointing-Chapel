Welcome to Our Church Family

We are delighted to have you visit our website! Impact Anointing Chapel has been a beacon of faith in the community since 1990, under the spiritual leadership of Archbishop Dr. Frank Ed Wood. We are a vibrant community of believers committed to winning souls and bringing salvation to all who seek the truth.

Whether you are searching for a church home or exploring your faith journey, we invite you to join us for worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. Come and experience the transforming power of God's love in our warm and welcoming congregation.
